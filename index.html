What is Ayurveda?

Ayurveda, literally meaning "knowledge of life," is an ancient system of natural healing that originated in India over 5,000 years ago. It is considered one of the world's oldest healing systems and focuses on maintaining health through a balanced approach to living.

Unlike modern medicine that often treats symptoms, Ayurveda addresses the root cause of illness by considering the whole person - body, mind, and spirit. It emphasizes prevention and treatment through lifestyle practices, dietary guidelines, herbal remedies, yoga, and meditation.

  • Manages nervous system
  • Influences creativity and flexibility

When Imbalanced:

  • Controls digestion and metabolism
  • Regulates body temperature
  • Manages hormones
  • Influences intelligence and understanding

Intelligent, focused, confident, and warm

When Imbalanced:
